I am interested in knowing if Leslie Lamport published any paper before the 80s. If so, what are they?
------------------
Yes, Leslie Lamport published several papers before the 80s. Here are some of his publications:

1. Title: A general construction for expressing repetition.
   Type: Article
   DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1145/988071.988076
   Year: 1979
   Published in: ACM SIGPLAN Notices

2. Title: A New Approach to Proving the Correctness of Multiprocess Programs.
   Type: Article
   DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1145/357062.357068
   Year: 1979
   Published in: ACM Trans. Program. Lang. Syst.

3. Title: How to Make a Multiprocessor Computer That Correctly Executes Multiprocess Programs.
   Type: Article
   DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1109/tc.1979.1675439
   Year: 1979
   Published in: IEEE Trans. Computers

4. Title: On the Proof of Correctness of a Calendar Program.
   Type: Article
   DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1145/359156.359160
   Year: 1979
   Published in: Commun. ACM

5. Title: The specification and proof of correctness of interactive programs.
   Type: Inproceedings
   DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1007/3-540-09541-1_37
   Year: 1979
   Published in: Mathematical Studies of Information Processing

Please note that these are only a few examples. Leslie Lamport has published numerous other papers before the 80s.
